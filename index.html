The New CLICK160

Honda Click 160 - Rizal Autozone presents the ideal ride for discerning riders: The New CLICK160.

Eye-Catching Good Looks
The New CLICK160 boasts a modern design with an angular front cowl, front fender, and side fairings. Its newly designed all-LED headlights and taillights not only enhance its striking appearance but also improve nighttime visibility, ensuring a safe ride wherever you go. Available in three stunning color variants—Matte Pearl Crater White, Matte Solar Red Metallic, and Matte Gunpowder Black Metallic—this bike perfectly combines style and sophistication, making you stand out on every journey.

Performance That Impresses
Equipped with a 4-Stroke, 4-Valve, SOHC, Liquid-Cooled eSP+ engine, the New CLICK160 delivers an impressive 11.3 kW of max power at 8,500 rpm and 13.8 Nm of max torque at 7,000 rpm. This power is perfect for navigating through the city or embarking on scenic rides. With a 5.5-liter fuel tank and an impressive fuel consumption rate of 46.7 km/L (based on the World Motorcycle Test Cycle), you can enjoy extended rides without frequent stops at the pump.

Control and Safety Features
Safety is paramount, and the New CLICK160 is equipped with Honda’s Combi Brake System (CBS), wider tires, and newly designed cast wheels for enhanced control. These features ensure you reach your destination safely, no matter the conditions. Additionally, the built-in USB charger keeps your devices powered, so you’re always ready to capture those unforgettable moments on the road. With a larger luggage box, you can easily carry essentials for spontaneous adventures, whether it's a day at the beach or a weekend getaway.

Secure and Convenient
Beyond its stylish design and impressive performance, the New CLICK160 comes with a range of features that make daily adventures more convenient and secure. The Smart Key System with Anti-Theft Alarm and Answer Back System provides peace of mind while the Idling Stop System helps conserve fuel, allowing you to save for your next adventure.

The New CLICK160 is more than just a motorcycle; it's a perfect companion for those who seek style, performance, and convenience on every ride.
